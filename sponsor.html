WHY SPONSOR US

NETWORK AND EXPOSURE

We are an interdisciplinary team with the diverse set of students who offer a deeper set of connections with IIT Bombay's staff, faculty, alumni, and mentors from ISRO and direct exposure to IIT Bombay's industry and research culture

BRAND ENHANCEMENT

Brand enhancement at multiple avenues like alumni conclaves, SA Cup competition, news articles, and social media handles by incorporating your names and logos on our goods, merchandise, and social media handles.

CSR AND TAX EXEMPTION

IITB Rocket team is a college technical team, so there will be tax rebate on contributions for scientific research under the relevant sections of the Income Tax Act. This also provides you a platform to show support for scientific innovation and research.

VISION

A team of 45+ students aiming at rocketry and propagating space sciences in India, and develop the STEM-oriented community. It's our vision, and you can help us in the journey!

WHY WE NEED SPONSORSHIP

MANUFACTURING

The IITB Rocket Team would require funds for manufacturing the rocket which includes the cost of building materials, testing equipment and lab charges .

ROCKET SHIPPING

The rocket needs to be packed appropriately after disassembling and needs to be transported to New Mexico for the competition.

COMPETITION LOGISTICS

Funding is required to cover airfare, ground travel, lodging, registration fees etc. for the team members.

MISCELLANEOUS

Team merchandise, printing, expense of various events, and outreach of the tech team which are essential to let people know more about us and our work.
